Clean Water Commitment

Project roles: Creative direction, illustration, animation, sound design

I was honored to collaborate with the folks over at the Lake Champlain Basin Program to create a series of informational animations focused on the phosphorus challenges facing Lake Champlain. I lead the creative direction on the project, which married playful illustrated environments with informational motion graphics to highlight the run-off challenges facing New England's largest lake.

Learn more regarding water pollution in Lake Champlain at cleanwatercommitment.org.
