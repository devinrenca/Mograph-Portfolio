Farm First: Flagship Animation

Project roles: Creative direction, illustration, animation

Farmers are an essential part of Vermont's workforce, but stress associated with the job can be overwhelming. That's where FarmFirst comes in, providing counseling, support, and financial advice to farmer's throughout the state.

In collaboration with Place Creative, I directed, illustrated and animated this flagship spot for the program. The final result succeeds in beautifully highlighting FarmFirst's mission in under 40 seconds.
