Hireability Vermont: Youth Program Overview

Project roles: Creative direction, illustration, animation

For young adults with disabilities, looking to enter the workforce poses a multitude of challenges. Hireability Vermont's youth program helps to break down these obstacles by providing essential career counseling and training.

Place Creative Company invited me in to provide the creative direction for this overview animation. From styleframes to the final animation, we built a diverse, empowered creative direction to give voice to this impactful program.
