Putting beautiful things
into motion.

Based in New England, Devin Renca specializes in playful motion design, animation & illustration. He brings years of creative experience to the table, both as a member of a studio team and as a freelance creative. A graduate of Champlain College, he earned his Bachelor of Fine Arts in Graphic Design.

With a strong D.I.Y. mentality, Devin finds fufillment no matter the medium. A camera lens, computer mouse or pen and paper are all viable creative tools. When not creating, he relaxes by jamming out to punk rock, goofing around with his pup Josie, or kicking around a soccer ball.

Devin is available to lend his expertise in motion design, animation, illustration and 3D visualization. Thanks for stopping by!
